# Arrival

You arrive at the Trojans on 23 Nov 189, at an aquatic station with an unpronounceable name that air-breathers simply call "The Fishbowl".

The aquatics here are culturally very different from the aquatics of Ceres, a fact that becomes known to you when Klahr is hauled off to a detention center within seconds of arrival.  The only information you have regarding this development is Klahr's brief message to Lou stating, *"I kind of expected this, it's fine. I'll be back in a few days"*, sent while station security crabs dragged him through the airlock.

Minutes after arrival, a very tall JIS operative arrived to escort Serenity to her "debriefing". 

While docking, the station's biotech, *==Manannán mac Lir==*, informs your group that it is ready to pull Lucky "and his friends" from the rogue station brain to prepare them for resleeving here at The Fishbowl.  Without access to Lucky's thoughts during the two weeks it took to arrive here, the group is rightfully concerned about what "and friends" could mean.

#### The Bubble Hotel
Your party has been given a suite in the penthouse level of the Bubble Hotel, one of the few places within the aquatic sphere that interfaces both the air-breathing and aquatic sides.

###### Guests
There are 3 guest suites attached to your rooms.  One is occupied by the now-awake Dr. Moros and his assistants, The Musus.  Another suite is occupied by the station liaison who will negotiate and facilitate any repairs, reloads, and dock work your ship needs.  This representative is a large crab who introduces himself as Cht!Trxt'k, name that sounds more like stepping on broken seashells than a sound you can actually vocalize.

# This Fishbowl
This station has a rounded, tetrahedral, water-filled space externally polished to a mirror shine with four spokes protruding radially.  These spokes contain a large industrial nano-forge, 2 well-equipped repair-refit spacedocks, and a small defunct habitation ring.  ***Silent Eternity*** is docked in the repair wing of the port-side spacedock awaiting work orders.

Your hotel is inside the central tetrahedron, at the edge of the central ocean cavity.  Your room looks out to an impressive coral reef surrounding a submerged city, decorated to appear like a ruined human city.  Numerous aquatics of several species swim past your rooms, clearly people-watching.  Arriving at your rooms, you quickly realize you weren't comped a luxury suite as you first thought.  You are a circus attraction.

This is your home...
 - while your ship gets a new engine (maybe more? up to you).  The station has a full, industrial nano-forge and skilled technicians.
 - while its decided what happens with the rogue station brain.
 - while data taken from the rogue station is parsed and processed.
 - while your friend(s?) resleeve.
 - while you figure out who, what, Moros and the Musus are, and what to do with them.
 - while Rama figures out what is next for your team.
