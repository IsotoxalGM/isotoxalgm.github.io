Destruction of the Venusian command cruiser, "*Where Shadows Speak and Tears Will Hide*"

# Proconsul - Militis Analysis

Brandmauer has finish the general analysis of the Proconsuls and Militis you brought to them.  Further analysis is possible if you direct the team toward a specific focus of inquery. 

#### Magister Militis 
The Magister Militis Combat Proxies are paired to a specific Proconsul unit similar to how the Grizzlies are paired to the Ranger power armor.  However, there are also several command channels identified as well.  It seems that one particular Proconsul suit is able to control a particular Militis, but something higher in heirarchy than that Proconsul has hardwired access to override the Proconsul's commands.  Additionally, there is even a super-override layer that seems capable of commanding all Militis even beyond first override layer.

Brandmauer can re-pair the Militis to another control unit, and even disable the overrides, but they can't be 100% certain that *all* overrides are disabled.  At least not until we get a better idea of the upper command structure for their elite armored units.

#### Proconsul
The Proconsul morphs are highly engineered pseudo-morphs, in that they cannot survive without being inside some synthetic wrapper, like the Proconsul armor.  Its clear from their design that the Proconsul morph can remove itself from the armor normally, but only to insert itself into some other exosuit.  We believe it likely that Proconsuls have several exosuit variations to cover their needs based on whatever assignment they are given for the day.  The Proconsul armor you brought seems to be their Light Power Armor variation.  There is evidence of a Heavy Power Armor unit as well as a lightwieght non-powered armor variant.  Evidence also suggests there are several more beyond these three, but we don't have enough data to determine what capabilities the undiscovered variants might have.

Due to the configuration and biological needs of the Proconsul pseudo-morphs, the Proconsul armor you captured is not suitable for use by normal morphology.

# Psychosurgeon Analysis 
Most of the egos recovered from the biotech unit taken from the rogue station have been processed through the Martian Egonet, most of which happened before Brandmauer was able to isolate or even question.  In hindsight, keeping the recovered egos out of a large Egonet until a proper debrief had been done might have been the better play, from a Firewall perspective, though certainly not from a civil right's perspective.  

Further investigation into those who were recovered will have to be done "in person" once they've resleeved and re-entered normal life.

==Gillardo Feliciano==.  Fled to the Venus Egonet.  We can't be 100% certain but many at Brandmauer believe this ego belongs to one of the Proconsuls you fought on the station.

==Boarding Team==. There is evidence that the two Proconsuls that boarded from the vessel *Where Shadows Speak and Tears Will Hide* were captured by the nearby Monolith mining station.

# Command Cruiser Propulsion Ring
Initial analysis of the ring shows it was designed and built by Monolith.  The ring itself is a power-delivery system that more efficiently separates engine thrust-nozzles from their powerplants.  It means more powerful engines can be placed further from their reactors.  Another step forward in making faster, larger capital ships, and possibly interstellar transports.

A bonus property of the ring's ability to precisely direct a high volume of power seems to give it the ability to push huge amounts of power into a very small space outside the ring itself, with high precision.  We believe it was the ring that was vaporizing your ordnance.  We aren't sure what might have happened if you attempted to board the ship before disabling the ring, but we know it wouldn't have been pretty for you.

Firewall has determined the stolen ring has an importance outside the scope of your current mission and has given it to another team.  You are directed to never again mention the ring, or its potential tech to anyone else, not even Brandmauer.
