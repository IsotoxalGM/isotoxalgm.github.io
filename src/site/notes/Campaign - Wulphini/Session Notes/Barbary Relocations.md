# Barbary Rock Refugees

Several of the Barbary Rock refugees have finished the preparations to reintegrate them into society in places where they can continue the work of Brandmauer's "official" work.

==Demetria Amuri== has been inserted into House Falconis as Personal Assistant to the attorney, ***Ksana Florig***.

==Captain Leotnyev== has been inserted into House Falconis as Security Lead (Transit) to the attorney, ***Ksana Florig***.

==Mariska Sharma== is enrolled in undergraduate studies at the Ganymede Polytechnical Institute Center for Health and Wellness.  Her story is she is retraining to gain certification in resleeving tech as a personal quest to uncover exactly "what happened to her".  There are no official investigations or even reports of what she's been through, which she is using as evidence of a "coverup".  She plans to be vocal about her trip to the Wulf Comet which tore her ego loose and left her disembodied for what felt like eternity.

==Santianna Pohl== has returned to Ceres to find her family and re-establish herself.  She has already revealed to them, truthfully, that she was essentially kidnapped, exploited, and had her morph messed with on some icy rock.  Her unspoken mission is to get in touch with a great uncle who she believes to be a highly ranked member of the Ten Tigers crime syndicate.  She intends to enlist his help in unraveling the coverup, perhaps even seeking vengeance, through nefarious methods.

==Dean Amuri== has entered the Martian Egonet via New Vegas where he is contracted to spend 18 months as an informorph handling cross-network comm traffic.  His story is this stint in the egonet is his way of overcoming emotional trauma stemming from a sleeve mishap involving proximity to Comet Wulf.

==Rish Lang== has enrolled in a apprenticeship program as a Drive Technician.  His first year will be spent in classroom studies and practical skills lab based on Callisto.

==Kerianne Lang== has taken employment in community youth care group on Callisto, where she has legal guardianship over Rish Lang.
