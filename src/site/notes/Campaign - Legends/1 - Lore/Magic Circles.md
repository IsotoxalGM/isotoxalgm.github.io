# Circle of Protection

Given by the formula: 
$$\left(\zeta(3)^{y} \cdot \varphi x\right)^{2}+y^{2}=1$$
Where: [Apéry's constant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ap%C3%A9ry%27s_constant "Apéry's constant"):
$$\zeta(3) = 1.2020569031...$$ 
And [Golden ratio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_ratio) phi: $$\varphi = 1.6180339887...$$

This equation is equal to 1 unit height of the caster.

Giving the figure:

The widest point of this circle being ~125% of the caster's height, roughly 7 to 8 feet in diameter.
The portion of the circle that extends below ground is about 75% of the caster's height, or about 50 to 58 inches.
